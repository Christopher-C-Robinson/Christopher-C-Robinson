BingoFlow

BingoFlow is an iOS app for scanning and managing bingo cards. The public site keeps the project page, support flow, and privacy policy under one clear path.

Open support Open privacy policy Back to projects

What it does

  • Scan printed bingo cards with the camera.
  • Create cards manually or generate them randomly.
  • Track called numbers and win patterns on-device.
  • Keep the release path public for TestFlight and App Store review.
