BingoFlow Privacy Policy

BingoFlow is a local bingo card management app. The app is designed to keep your card data on your device and does not require an account.

Information We Process

  • Camera input: used only when you scan a physical bingo card. Image processing and OCR happen on device to detect the card and recognize numbers.
  • App data: card lists, marked numbers, theme preferences, and other in-app state are stored locally on your device.
  • Motion access: used only for the app's subtle UI effects on supported devices.

Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

  • No account registration information.
  • No advertising identifiers or analytics in the app codebase.
  • No server-side storage of your bingo card data by the app.
  • No sale of personal information.

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on Apple platform services for distribution, TestFlight, and App Store review. Those services are controlled by Apple and are separate from the app's runtime behavior.
